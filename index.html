Privacy Policy

Introduction

BlendBrain is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. This Privacy Policy explains how BlendBrain collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

When you use the BlendBrain App, we may collect the following types of information:

  1. Personal Information: When you register and create an account on BlendBrain, we collect your mobile number for authentication purposes. We also collect the One-Time Password (OTP) verification sent to your mobile number. Additionally, when you fill out your profile information, we may collect your name, email address, profile picture, and any other information you choose to provide.
  2. User Generated Content: BlendBrain allows users to create and upload content, such as documents, posts, comments, and likes. We may collect and store this user-generated content to provide the services offered by the App.
  3. Usage Information: We collect information about how you use the BlendBrain App, including your interactions with other users, document uploads, searches, and engagement with posts, likes, and comments.
  4. Device Information: We may collect device-specific information, such as your device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users and provide access to the BlendBrain App.
  • To personalize and improve the user experience.
  • To facilitate communication and interaction between users.
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends to improve our services.
  • To respond to user inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

BlendBrain does not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our rights.
  • To service providers who assist us in operating the App and providing the services.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. If you wish to delete your account or request the removal of specific information, please contact us at your@email.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at your@email.com.
